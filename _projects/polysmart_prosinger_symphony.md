**An AI innovation that composes, orchestrates, and performs full orchestral symphonies tailored to your mood, instruments, and musical ideas.**
- Developed by our expert team from the Dept. of Computing at PolyU, as part of the **PolySmart** intelligent-space ecosystem.
- Integrates **Generative AI for Music** (MusicLang, Anticipatory Music Transformer, YuE), **symbolic orchestration**, **neural audio synthesis** (ViolinDiff, DDPM), **source separation** (Demucs), and **professional stem mixing**.
- The prototype runs as an interactive web demo with a guided step-by-step UI; generated symphonies can be previewed, downloaded, and explored stem-by-stem.


## What can PolySmart Prosinger Symphony do?

PolySmart Prosinger Symphony offers **three creative modes** on one platform: compose a short orchestral piece from scratch, transform a piano MIDI into a full symphony, or turn an AI-written song into a vocal-plus-orchestra performance.


### 1. Piano MIDI to Symphony

Turn a **solo piano MIDI** (preset classics or your own upload) into a **multi-instrument orchestral symphony** with expressive neural rendering and studio-quality mixing.

Pick **orchestral instruments**, a **mood**, and **tempo**—the system composes fresh multi-track MIDI and renders a complete symphony in about ten seconds.
