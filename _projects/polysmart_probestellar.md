## The Beautiful 3-Body Universe You Should Explore in PolySmart ProbeStellar 

PolySmart ProbeStellar is an interactive app that lets you explore a collection of remarkable three-body orbits: configurations where three stars pull on one another and eventually return to where they started, tracing the same path again and again, forever.

Each little cluster of three glowing stars is its own miniature dance. Some moves are simple; others are intricate and surprising. Together they form a vast constellation you can fly through, zoom into, and watch in motion.
