**An AI innovation that places your portrait into iconic PolyU campus backgrounds with professional artistic styling—tailored to your photo, location, and creative vision.**
- Developed by our expert team from the Dept. of Computing at PolyU, as part of the **PolySmart** intelligent-space ecosystem.
- Integrates **Generative AI for Images** (Seedream 5.0), **Computer Vision** (face-aware compositing, lighting harmonization), **interactive web UI**, **queue-based generation**, **QR-code mobile sharing**, and **email delivery**.
- The prototype runs as a guided step-by-step web demo in the Smart Space; visitors upload a selfie, pick a campus scene and art style, and receive a high-resolution AI photo in about one minute.


## What can PolySmart Persona do?

PolySmart Persona offers a **five-step creative workflow** on one platform: upload your portrait, choose where you appear on campus, pick an artistic look, optionally add a custom description, then generate, download, scan, or email your personalized image.

### 1. Upload Your Photo

Start with a favorite portrait—the system preserves your facial identity while transforming the scene and artistic rendering around you.


### 2. Select Your PolyU Background

Choose from **eight iconic PolyU locations**—from the Jockey Club Innovation Tower and P504 Student Lab (RC-DSAI) to the Great Lawn, Clock Square, Library, and Garden Restaurant. The AI places you naturally into the scene with matched lighting and perspective.


### 3. Choose Your Artistic Style

Transform your photo with **twelve curated styles**—from Cyberpunk and Studio Ghibli to Van Gogh oil painting, watercolor, Japanese anime, magazine cover, and vintage film.


### 4. Customize & Generate

Optionally add a **natural-language description** for extra creative direction, then generate your personalized image. A **queue system** keeps the Smart Space demo smooth when many visitors are creating photos at once.


### 5. Download, Share & Explore

**Download** your high-resolution image on the kiosk, **scan a QR code** to save it on your phone, **email** it to yourself, or browse the community **Persona Gallery** of past creations.
